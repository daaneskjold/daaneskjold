About the game. I am a huge basketball fan (and anime fan but that is other story). And I love the Miami Heat. The game takes you through a few paths depending on what you choose, and if you choose the path that will eventually lead you to my favorite team (the Miami Heat), on every layer you get an alert message with some encouraging -you are going through the right path- texts, that are different from all other paths which eventually show nothing. I think it adds a nice twist, because the actual game is not me guessing the team (which is pointless), is YOU going through the right flow. The bad thing? It has no replayability once you figured out the answer, which you can do immediatly just by looking at the code :P . Anyway! Just an example lol.
